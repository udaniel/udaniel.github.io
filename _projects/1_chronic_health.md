### Project Overview
This project focuses on the development and external validation of non-invasive machine learning tools for identifying subjects to be screened for chronic conditions, such as Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD), prediabetes, and diabetes. 

By leveraging patient-accessible data, we build scalable, home-based early warning systems that empower individuals and alleviate burdens on healthcare systems.

### Key Outputs
* **Publication:** *Enhancing Prediabetes and Diabetes Detection Through a Machine Learning-Enabled Self-Assessment Approach* (Journal of Clinical Epidemiology, 2026).
* **Publication:** *MERWACS: Development and External Validation of a Non-Invasive Machine Learning Tool for Identifying Subjects to be Screened for CKD* (PLOS Digital Health, 2026).
* **Presentations:** Presented at European Public Health Conference 2025 (Helsinki), AIMed 2025 (San Diego), the 14th Nordic Public Health Conference (Gothenburg).
